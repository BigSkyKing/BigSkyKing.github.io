Long Biography

Jiachen Xu, born in 2000 in China, produced his first sound on violin at the age of four, and showed his strong interest and talent in music as a child. And he decided to devote himself to music in his high school period after hearing inspiring recordings of Jascha Heifetz performing the 24th Paganini Caprice and Salvatore Accardo playing La Campanella and being further inspired by countless recordings of Hilary Hahn and James Ehnes. In 2018, he enrolled Shanghai Conservatory of Music, one of the top conservatories in China. He won People’s Scholarship of Shanghai Conservatory of Music three times as an undergraduate student and received the Second Prize of Professional Group in 4th National String Competition. in 2020, as a member of Yan String Quartet, he participated in an event jointly organized by the Shanghai Conservatory of Music and the Shanghai Longhua Martyr Cemetery to demonstrate the power of music and pay tribute to heroes as well as cherished the memory of history. The quartet also performed concerts at many venues including Shanghai Grand Theater and Shangyin Opera House. As a orchestra member in Shanghai Conservatory of Music he performed at Shanghai Grand Theater for Martha Argerich Shanghai Concert in 2019 and attended the New Era Song of the Chinese Long March National Tour. During his time at Manhattan School of Music, he participated in events like Broadway musical Beauty and the Beast with musical director James Dodgsons, Concert conducted by New York Philharmonic associate conductor Joshua Gersen and did interesting performance with Jazz BigBand. In his last year of bachelor, he began to teach violin and music theory as well to gain broader career experience. As a master's student, he continued to perform as a member of orchestra and in there he also studied creating MIDI music and created some of his own work in many kinds of styles. Apart from music, he enjoys absorbing various aspect of knowledge, including Newest Technology , Financial Market, and also Philosophy, and he believes people should never stop learning new things and exploring the universe.

Short Biography

Jiachen Xu, born in 2000 in China, produced his first sound on violin from the age of four and showed his strong interest and talent in music as a child. He learned in Shanghai Conservatory of music for bachelor's degree, before enrolling as a master's student at the Manhattan School of Music.
